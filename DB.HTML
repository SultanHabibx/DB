Our Sources

Where Our Products Come From

We import from carefully selected origins chosen for their product quality, food safety standards, and longstanding trade relationships.

Myanmar

Primary — Frozen Fish

The heart of our business. Myanmar's rich coastal and river fisheries supply us with a wide variety of premium frozen fish species — our core product and biggest focus.

India

Beef Imports

Quality halal beef sourced from certified Indian suppliers. Every shipment is accompanied by full halal certification and SFDA-approved import documentation.

Local KSA

Fresh Chicken

Supporting local production, we source fresh chicken from licensed Saudi farms. Short supply chains mean faster delivery and maximum freshness for our customers.
